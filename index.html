Our Programs

Every Age, A Unique Journey

Our meditation programs are thoughtfully designed for each developmental stage, ensuring every child receives guidance that resonates with their world.

Classes 2–2

🌱 Playful Roots

Joyful, sensory-rich activities that plant the first seeds of awareness. Through playful breathing games, nature sounds, and gentle storytelling, little ones learn to pause, feel, and wonder — building a foundation of calm curiosity.

  • Breathing games & body awareness
  • Guided imagination journeys
  • Nature-based mindfulness play
Classes 3–5

🧯 Focused Minds

Building concentration and emotional vocabulary during the curious middle years. Children explore structured mindfulness exercises, attention anchoring, and reflection journaling — sharpening focus while nurturing kindness toward themselves and others.

  • Concentration & attention exercises
  • Emotion naming & journaling
  • Mindful listening & observation
Classes 6–8

💜 Emotional Harmony

Navigating the whirlwind of adolescence with grace. Pre-teens develop emotional resilience through guided meditations, stress-relief techniques, and peer mindfulness circles — learning to understand their feelings and respond rather than react.

  • Emotional regulation practices
  • Stress & anxiety management
  • Peer mindfulness circles
Classes 9–12

✨ Inner Wisdom

Deep self-inquiry for young adults preparing for life's big transitions. Teens engage in contemplative practices, philosophical reflection, and purpose-finding meditations — cultivating clarity, confidence, and an unshakeable inner compass.

  • Deep contemplative practices
  • Purpose & values exploration
  • Exam stress & life transition tools
Why Meditate?

The Gift of Stillness

Research shows that meditation helps children thrive — in school, at home, and within themselves.

Better Focus

Improved concentration and attention span help children excel in academics and activities they love.

Emotional Balance

Children learn to manage big emotions, reduce anxiety, and build healthy coping mechanisms for life.

Stronger Connections

Mindful children develop empathy, kindness, and deeper relationships with family and friends.

About Us

Where Ancient Wisdom Meets Young Hearts

Sampoorn Anand (meaning "Complete Bliss") is a heartfelt initiative dedicated to bringing the transformative power of meditation to school-aged children.

We believe every child deserves the tools to navigate their inner world with confidence. Our programs blend time-tested contemplative practices with modern, child-friendly approaches — making mindfulness accessible, enjoyable, and deeply meaningful for every age group.

Led by experienced meditation practitioners and child development specialists, we create safe, nurturing spaces where young minds learn to breathe, reflect, and bloom.
